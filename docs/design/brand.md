# Brand — Bellring

## Name
**Bellring** — the metaphor: ringing the sales-floor bell when a deal closes. Chrome extension + future web dashboard. Domain: TBD (`bellring.io` / `bellring.app` — decide pre-launch). Paired with `bellring-server` (backend on Render).

## One-liner
Celebrate every closed deal — cross-team, in the browser, without leaving the CRM tab.

## Story
Sales teams lose the emotional punch of closing when wins happen in silos — the closer knows, the CRM logs it, the team misses it. Bellring is the tiny browser-native moment of shared joy: when a deal closes in Salesforce/HubSpot, every teammate's browser pings a celebration and shows who won what.

The name **Bellring** is the sales-floor tradition — when a rep closed, someone rang the bell, the floor cheered. Bellring makes that possible in distributed/remote teams too. The product itself is the celebration artifact — little bell animations, teammate congratulations, weekly leaderboard recap.

## Mission
- Restore the emotional high of closing to teams that don't share a physical sales floor.
- Integrate with CRMs (Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive) without friction.
- Make every close visible without being creepy — celebratory, never surveillance.

## Visual voice
**Energetic + grounded.** Axis points:
- **Energetic**: bell-yellow primary, motion-rich celebrations (confetti, bell-ring animations, teammate-avatars zooming in).
- **Grounded**: restrained at rest. Default UI is calm Vagary-dark; celebration moments are loud but rare.
- **Respectful of context**: sales reps work in call-heavy environments — sound is OFF by default, animation respects focus-mode, never blocks the CRM.
- **Chrome-native**: the extension UI lives inside popup / overlay; design must feel like it belongs in Chrome, not fight it.

## Audience
- **Primary**: B2B SaaS sales teams, 5–50 reps. Salesforce/HubSpot users.
- **Secondary**: RevOps / sales enablement — the person who installs Bellring org-wide.
- **Tertiary**: startup founders wanting team-wide celebration before they have a formal sales floor.

NOT: individual salespeople (the value is team-wide visibility). NOT: enterprise sales cycles (too slow for weekly celebration cadence). NOT: B2C.

## Competitors / differentiation
- vs. Salesforce Chatter / HubSpot Feed — built-in but boring; no celebration layer.
- vs. Slack win-channels — manual, reps forget to post. Bellring is automatic.
- vs. Gong / Chorus — conversation intelligence, different job. Bellring is purely the celebration.
- vs. Sparky.ai / similar gamification tools — those go heavy on leaderboard + points; Bellring is lighter, more about the moment of shared joy.

Differentiator: **browser-native + CRM-automatic + celebration-first**. The intersection owns a real lane.
